Child Safety Topics

  • Online Safety: Tips for safe internet use, including avoiding strangers online, recognizing scams, and protecting personal information.
  • Stranger Danger: Important guidelines to stay safe around strangers, including trusting instincts and seeking help when necessary.
  • Emergency Contacts: Always keep emergency contacts accessible. Teach children whom to call in case of an emergency.
  • Home Safety: Ensure that children understand basic home safety practices, like handling kitchen appliances carefully and recognizing hazards.

Bullying Prevention

  • Identifying Bullying: Learn the signs of bullying, such as changes in mood, avoidance of school, and physical injuries.
  • Communication: Encourage open conversations with children about their day and experiences at school.
  • Stand Up to Bullies: Teach assertiveness and safe ways to address bullying without escalating situations.

Emergency Procedures

  • Fire Safety: Teach children to follow fire safety rules, including knowing emergency exits and practicing drills.
  • Calling 112: Train children on when and how to call emergency services for quick assistance.
  • First Aid Basics: Show children basic first aid techniques for common injuries like cuts, scrapes, and burns.
